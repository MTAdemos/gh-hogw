Juilliard415: Breaking out of Boxes
March 2, 2011
During March Christopher will be making a special appearance at The Juilliard School in New York where he will conduct members of the student period-instrument ensemble, Juilliard415, in a program of Haydn and Geminiani. Juilliard Master's student, Luke Conklin, recently spoke with Christopher about the forthcoming concert in an interview for The Juilliard Journal. Read the full article here.

 

[N.B. Francesco Geminiani's Concerto No.3 in B minor after Corelli Op.3 No.4 was composed in 1735; the Concerto No.2 in D major from ‘Select Harmony’ in 1736; and the Concerto No.1 in D major from 2 Unison Concertos in 1761.]
