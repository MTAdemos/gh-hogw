Christopher on WPRB Princeton radio
October 11, 2011
This Thursday the student-run radio station WPRB Princeton will be broadcasting a programme featuring Christopher in conversation with Teri Towe. American audiences can follow the broadcast of 'Towe on Thursday' live from 06:00 to 11:00 Eastern Time on 103.3 FM. Alternatively, the programme is available live over the Internet on the WPRB Princeton website.
