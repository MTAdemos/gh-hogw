Christopher receives an honorary doctorate from the RCM
May 14, 2013

The award of Doctor of Music, honoris causa, was presented to him by His Royal Highness The Prince of Wales, as part of the President's Visit to the RCM, in recognition of "his contribution to music and the Royal College of Music". For more information on the day, including other awards presented, please see the RCM website
