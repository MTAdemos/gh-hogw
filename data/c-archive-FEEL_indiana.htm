FEELguide.com on 'Indiana Hogwood'
January 31, 2012
The Canadian online magazine FEELguide.com recently published a piece about Christopher's involvement in the first publication of a newly discovered Brahms piano piece, which he titled Albumblatt. Christopher is colourfully depicted as "one-part world renowned musicologist and one-part Indiana Jones", escaping ancient ruins from unfathomable terrors, fighting off venomous snakes and saving damsels in distress, all to bring us a newly discovered work by Brahms.
