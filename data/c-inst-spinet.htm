Spinet

Maker Thomas Hancock
Manufactured London, 1732
Description Bentside spinet with mitred tail. Walnut veneered casework with stringing and crossbanding (in ash?) on the interior. Solid walnut lid. Raised on a turned trestle stand.
Inscriptions Thomas Hancock London in ink on name batten, and 1732 on the FF keylever (reproduced on new lever).
Specification One 8', strung in brass.
Pitch A=415
Compass GG–g3
References
Boalch3 number: HANCOCK, T. 1723(A) (confused entry, some of which refers to this instrument).
Restoration 1978 Chris Nobbs
