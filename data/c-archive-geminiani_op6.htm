Pictures from Christopher's latest Poznan appearance
April 2, 2013

The concert in Poznan on 22 March, repeated in Łódź on 24 March, comprised Bach's Brandenburg Concerto No. 3, BWV 1048, with cadenza by Penderecki, and Fuga (Ricercata) a 6 voci BWV 1079, arranged by Webern, as well as Stravinsky's Pulcinella (complete ballet), choreographed by Ewa Wycichowska and danced by soloists of the Polish Dance Theatre.

Christopher's next engagement in Poznan will be at the end of this month, where he will conduct a programme of Mendelssohn, including his violin concerto with Henning Kraggerud as soloist.
