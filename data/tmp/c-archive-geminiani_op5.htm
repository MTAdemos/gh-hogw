First Practical Editions of Geminiani Opera Omnia
February 20, 2013
The first four volumes of the ‘practical edition’ of the Complete Works of Francesco Geminiani have just appeared from Ut Orpheus, Bologna.

They offer the Op. 5 sonatas for cello and basso continuo in two volumes (Sonatas I-III: €23.95 and Sonatas IV-VI: €19.95) and the same sonatas in their violin and basso continuo versions by Geminiani (hitherto unavailable in a compete modern edition) with Sonatas I-III at €23.95 and Sonatas IV-VI at €20.95.

All these critical editions contain score and parts, including a continuo realisation by Valeria Tarsetti.
