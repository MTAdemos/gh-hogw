Christopher featured on Radio 3's In Tune
May 23, 2013
Christopher was interviewed by Sean Rafferty on Tuesday's In Tune, ahead of his next project conducting Handel's Imeneo with the AAM in Madrid and London. Other guests included Peter Donohoe and Elisabeth Meister.
