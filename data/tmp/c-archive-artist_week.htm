Christopher is Artist of the Week on Radio 3!
March 18, 2013
He was also much surprised and pleased to hear his recording of Stravinsky's Pulcinella with the Saint Paul Chamber Orchestra whilst driving to catch the plane to conduct the very same piece in Poznan this week!


Recordings of Christopher's will be featured on Rob Cowan's Essential Classics programme every morning from 9am to 12pm. Included so far have been: Arne Overture in G minor (AAM with Christopher conducting), Byrd's John, come kisse me now from the Fitzwilliam Virginal Book (Christopher on virginals), Telemann's Concerto in E for flute, oboe d'amore and viola d'amore (AAM with Christopher conducting), and Stravinsky's Pulcinella which Christopher heard in the car.

Please do tune in each morning, and check the Radio 3 website for more details!
