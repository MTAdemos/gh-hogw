Square Pianos

Maker Johannes Pohlmann
Manufactured London, 1773
Description English square piano with Zumpe action. In mahogany case with inlayed lines on a trestle stand with moulded rails.
Inscription Johannes Pohlmann Londini fecit 1773 on name batten.
Specification Bichord stringing on a single bridge. Zumpe action (simple stossmechanik with sprung overdampers). Three stop levers at bass end of keyboard operating: Treble dampers, Bass dampers, and mute or 'buff'.
Pitch A=430
Compass GG-f3
