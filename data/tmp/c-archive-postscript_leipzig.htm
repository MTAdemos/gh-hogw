Postscript to the Leipzig competition
August 13, 2012
Christopher writes from Dubrovnik about the tote distributed to each jury member in Leipzig last month: "As I suspected, the much despised green satchel proved absolutely wonderful as a beach bag in Croatia. Not only has such a colour never been seen before on the Dalmatian coast, but the German text appeared to add territorial strength, and our beach-towels were never once disturbed."
