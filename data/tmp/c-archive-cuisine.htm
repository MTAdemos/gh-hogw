Martinů's La Revue de Cuisine Piano Suite
March 12, 2012
In 2009, Christopher produced a critical edition of Bohuslav Martinů's La Revue de Cuisine, the first time the complete ballet music had been published since being written in 1927. Martinů also arranged the ballet music as a suite for piano solo, which has just been published by Alphonse Leduc, as edited by Christopher.
