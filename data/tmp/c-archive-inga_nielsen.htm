Inga Nielsen CD featuring Christopher
November 26, 2007

We would like to offer our congratulations to Inga Nielsen on the release of her new CD marking the thirty-fifth anniversary of her debut as a professional singer. Inga Nielsen Voices – a compilation demonstrating her wide ranging repertoire – features a recording of 'My Vengeance' from Handel's Athalia made by Inga with Christopher and the Danish National Symphony Orchestra in 1993.

We were very sorry to read of Inga Nielsen's death, on 10 February 2008, aged 61.

To buy a copy of the CD, visit the Chandos website.
