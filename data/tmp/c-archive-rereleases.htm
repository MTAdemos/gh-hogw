Re-releases on Decca L'Oiseau-Lyre label
November 26, 2007

Decca has re-released five of Christopher's recordings from its former L'Oiseau-Lyre collection. The five were featured on the September highlights section of the Decca website:

Arne: 8 Overtures - FIRST INTERNATIONAL RELEASE ON CD
Haydn: Symphonies Volume 1
Purcell: Songs and Airs
Rebel and Destouches: Les Elémens
Vivaldi: Four Seasons


For more information on the re-releases visit the Decca website
