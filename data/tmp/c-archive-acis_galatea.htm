Acis and Galatea DVD release
March 9, 2010
Acis and Galatea hits the shelves.

In April 2009 Christopher conducted the Orchestra of the Age of Enlightenment at the Royal Opera House in a collaboration with the Opera House and the Royal Ballet, performing Dido and Aeneas and Acis and Galatea.

The performances were recorded live for DVD release and the first, Dido and Aeneas, was released in October 2009. This month sees the release of the second in the series, Acis and Galatea.

If you would like to buy the DVD of either Acis and Galatea or Dido and Aeneas then go to the Opus Arte website.
