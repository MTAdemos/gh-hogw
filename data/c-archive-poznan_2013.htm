Christopher encourages the Poznan Philharmonic to use historical instruments in Mendelssohn
May 1, 2013

The all-Mendelssohn programme included his Overture from "The Fair Melusine" Op.32, Violin Concerto in E minor Op.64 with Henning Kraggerud (obscured in the background) as the soloist, and his Symphony No.5 in D major "Reformation". Christopher's next engagement with the Poznan Philharmonic will be in June, and will include works by Haydn and Martinu.
